**Pattern for all XP-granting actions (per CONTEXT.md locked decision + RESEARCH.md Pattern 1):**
After every business action, execute this sequence:
1. Perform the business action (completeJob, addClient, etc.)
2. `gameStore.addXP(amount)` -- awards XP + auto-calculates level + HustleBucks
3. `gameStore.updateStreak()` -- updates consecutive day tracking
4. `checkBadges(...)` + `earnBadge()` for each result -- checks all unearned badges
5. `showXPToast(amount)` via useCelebration context -- shows floating toast
(Level-up and badge celebrations are auto-detected by CelebrationProvider's subscribe listener)
